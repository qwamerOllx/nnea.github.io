About

I am a passionate Computer Scientist with 3 years of experience in the fields of Software Engineering, Cybersecurity, and Web Development; developing and implementing innovative software and website solutions, and protecting business organizations from cyber-attacks. I am dedicated to delivering software solutions that drive business success. I am eager to learn and contribute my expertise to a forward-thinking organization that values innovation, teamwork, and continuous learning.

I also work with the Team in charge of Security Operations. I worked as a Red Teamer and later on advanced to work as a Blue Teamer. Also, I acquired a fair knowledge in Penetration Testing which enabled me to work as Gray Hat Hacker.
I collaborated with the product team to design school management software, solved complex problems using the latest in cloud, mobile, and web technologies. I built a robust web application using frameworks and technologies at GCTU.
